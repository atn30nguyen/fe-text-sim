Welcome to an online Fire Emblem Text Simulator

What is this?

This is a web-based text simulator for games in Nintendo's Fire Emblem franchise.

How does it work?

Simply click on the upper left section of the simulator to upload an image for the left side...

Then do the same with the upper right section of the simulator to upload an image for the right side...

Then click on the name and dialogue box to edit what's going on in the conversation.
